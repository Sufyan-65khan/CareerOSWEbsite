FAQ

Frequently asked
questions

Everything you need to know about CareerOS.

Is CareerOS free to use?

Yes! CareerOS has a free tier that includes your AI Career Score, basic resume analysis, and access to the Job Match Engine. You can get a lot of value without spending a rupee. Paid plans unlock unlimited scans, LinkedIn optimization, unlimited interview practice, and more advanced features.

How does AI interview practice work?

You tell CareerOS the role and company you're targeting. Our AI then conducts a realistic mock interview — asking you questions typical for that specific role, including behavioural, situational, and technical questions. You can respond by typing or voice. After each answer, the AI gives you detailed feedback on content quality, structure (STAR method), clarity, and what to improve. Sessions typically last 20–40 minutes.

Do you guarantee a job placement?

No. CareerOS is a career improvement and coaching platform — not a placement agency. We provide tools, AI guidance, and insights to maximise your chances of landing the right job. Job outcomes depend on many factors including the job market, your qualifications, and how consistently you use the platform. We're confident our tools significantly improve your chances, but we cannot guarantee specific outcomes.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings — no questions asked, no cancellation fees. Your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, you'll be moved to the free tier and retain access to your account and data.

How accurate is the AI Career Score?

The AI Career Score is generated by analysing your resume against thousands of successful job applications in your industry, comparing your LinkedIn completeness against high-performing profiles, and benchmarking your interview readiness. It's been validated against real hiring outcomes. While no score can predict every employer's decision, users who raise their score by 15+ points report a 3x increase in recruiter responses on average.

Is my resume data safe?

Absolutely. Your resume is encrypted with AES-256 and stored securely. We never share your data with third parties, recruiters, or employers without your explicit consent. Your data is never used to train AI models. You can delete all your data permanently at any time. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

What file formats does the resume analyzer support?

We support PDF, DOCX (Word), and plain text (.txt) formats. We recommend uploading a PDF for the most accurate parsing. Files must be under 10MB. If your resume is in a different format, you can paste the text directly into our editor.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI, net banking, and popular wallets. All payments are processed securely through Razorpay. We do not store any card or payment details on our servers.
